Welcome to Strength Forge

Located in the very middle of the city centre, our South-East Playmouth gym is easy to access from anywhere in the local area.

The Strength Forge gym is a modern gym consisting of 3 training areas.

The first for cardio training, equipped with treadmills, elliptical cross trainers and bicycles from Matrix, SportsArt and Bellagio.

The second includes easy-to-use professional Nautilus and Marbo strength training devices.

The Strength Forge also has a separate room with free weights for all those who prefer basic, free exercises.

The Strength Forge is a place where you will use all your time effectively, achieving your training goals.

Amenities at the Strength Forge

Cardio equipment

A wide range of cardio machines to help you maximise your fitness.

Free weights

Designated free weights area with a wide range of dumbells, free weight bars, and benches to help you strengthen your muscles.

Locker room

Modern locker room with showers to store your belongings.

Sauna

Sauna aid in recovery after intense physical activity.

Classes

ENHANCING The classes that shape an attractive and athletic figure. They improve the condition and strengthen all muscle groups.

CARDIO Cardio training helps reduce body fat and has a beneficial effect on the cardiovascular system. It releases "feel good" hormones that will help ease bad mood.

PRO-HEALTH Classes reducing the effects of overloads of the musculoskeletal system, make muscles more flexible and reduce their tension, and improve the stabilisation of the spine and peripheral joints.

CHOREOGRAPHY The classes, along with energetic music, are a pleasant form of shaping the figure and improving the work of the heart. Exercises allow relieving stress after a hard day.
