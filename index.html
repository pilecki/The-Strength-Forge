About us

The Strength Forge gym is a modern gym consisting of 3 training areas. The first for cardio training, equipped with treadmills, elliptical cross trainers and bicycles from Matrix, SportsArt and Bellagio. The second includes easy-to-use professional Nautilus and Marbo strength training devices. The Strength Forge also has a separate room with free weights for all those who prefer basic, free exercises. The Strength Forge is a place where you will use all your time effectively, achieving your training goals.
